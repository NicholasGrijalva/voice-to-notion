God changes our hearts through repentance, helping us see people as children of God and approach them with care and kindness. This change in heart leads to respectful behavior and guides life choices, making decisions feel less scary and more aligned with our purpose.

Repentance means changing one's heart, not just behavior.
Seeing people as children of God changes how we approach them.
A changed heart leads to respectful, caring, and kind behavior.
God's guidance helps with life choices, including marriage and vocation.
